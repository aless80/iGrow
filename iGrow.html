The graph shows the average weight in Kg of a baby between 0 and 200 weeks of age. The data was downloaded from the World Health Organization database.

Step 1 Add a baby: click on Edit babies. In the pop up dialog fill in the Name, Gender and Birthdate.

Step 2 Add, Edit or Remove a weight measurement: click on the "Edit measures" button.

You can zoom in the graph or click on the data to delete it, or hover over it to see more information.

To add a measurement on the table, click on "Add a measure".

To edit or remove a line on the table, select the line and click on "Edit measure" or "Delete measure".

On the section appearing on the botton indicate the baby's weight on a certain date and optionally a comment, then confirm or cancel your edits

Click on "Save & Close" or "Cancel"" to apply or ignore the edits. The data will be plotted on the graph.

You can export the data for each individual baby by clicking on "Export to file"
